About Digital Defence Club

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute Of Technology's Premier Cybersecurity Organization

The Digital Defence Club is a student-run organization dedicated to advancing cybersecurity education and awareness. As part of Chaitanya Bharathi Institute Of Technology, we organize national-level events to promote cybersecurity skills among students across India.

Our mission is to create a platform where students can learn, practice, and compete in cybersecurity challenges in a supportive and educational environment. We believe in fostering innovation and collaboration to address the growing cybersecurity challenges facing our digital world.
